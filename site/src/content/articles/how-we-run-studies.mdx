## Key findings

- This is a demonstration of the article format, rendered from MDX with a
  figure produced by the analysis toolkit. **All data on this page is
  synthetic.**
- Every real study follows the same structure: key findings up top, methods
  in brief, results, an explicit claims section, and data downloads.

## Why we asked

Real articles open with the research question, who it matters to, and the
mechanistic prior — what we predicted before touching the data, and why.

## Methods in brief

Real articles report the unit of analysis, the sample ("N citations evaluated
in this study"), the model in one paragraph, and link to the frozen
pre-registered spec on GitHub.

## What we can and cannot claim

This section is mandatory in every real article. It states the conditional
the design actually measured, what the design is blind to, and the achieved
equivalence bound — "we could have detected an effect of X; we found none
larger than Y."

## Data and reproducibility

Real articles link the anonymized dataset (with its datasheet and license),
the frozen spec commit, and the analysis code. See the
[methodology](/methodology) page for the standing commitments.
