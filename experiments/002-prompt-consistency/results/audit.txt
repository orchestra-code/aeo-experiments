## Audit B — outcome-label meaning

- 'brand recommended' = canonical brand whose alias matches the cleaned answer
  markdown (URLs stripped, word-boundary, longest-alias-first) — brands.py::extract_brands.
- 'domain cited' = registered domain of a normalized URL in result.sources[]
  (search_results[] are SERP extras and are EXCLUDED) — 01_features.py.
- 'grounding tokens' = stopword-filtered token union over result.fan_out_queries
  — aeo_research.overlap.token_set.
